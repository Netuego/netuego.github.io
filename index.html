Privacy Policy for Weather Fox

Last updated: June 27, 2026

Thank you for using Weather Fox.

This Privacy Policy explains what information the application may access, how it is used, and your choices regarding your data.

Information We Collect

Weather Fox does not require user registration and does not create personal accounts.

The app may access:

  • Your device location (only if you grant permission) to provide weather forecasts for your current location.
  • The cities you manually search for in order to display weather information.

How We Use Information

The information is used solely to:

  • Display current weather conditions and forecasts.
  • Automatically determine your location when permission is granted.
  • Improve the reliability and performance of the weather service.

Weather Data

Weather information is obtained through the Weather Fox backend service, which retrieves forecast data from Apple WeatherKit.

Data Storage

Weather Fox does not permanently store your precise location on its servers.

The application may temporarily cache weather data on your device and on the backend server to improve performance and reduce unnecessary requests.

Analytics

Weather Fox does not use advertising networks.

Anonymous technical information, such as server errors or performance metrics, may be collected to maintain and improve the service. This information cannot be used to identify individual users.

Third-Party Services

Weather Fox may use Apple services, including:

  • Apple WeatherKit
  • Apple Maps (for city search)

These services are governed by Apple's own privacy policies.

Your Choices

You may:

  • Deny or revoke location permission at any time in your device settings.
  • Search for cities manually instead of sharing your current location.

Children's Privacy

Weather Fox is not directed specifically toward children and does not knowingly collect personal information from children.

Changes to This Policy

This Privacy Policy may be updated from time to time. The latest version will always be available at this page.
